  - instead: stage-level SIMD redesign and benchmarking of chaotic image encryption pipelines

- Identified the main empirical story:
  - traditional chaotic image encryption is slow because of floating-point maps, sort-based permutation, and serial diffusion chains
  - SIMD-native redesigns improve replaceable stages
  - AES-CTR and ChaCha20 remain the correct cryptographic baselines
  - chaotic variants are better framed as image-domain transformation and research prototypes
